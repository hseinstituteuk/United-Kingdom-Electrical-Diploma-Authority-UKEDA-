Frequently Asked Questions

Which institutions participate in this verification system?

We currently verify qualifications from all JBM-accredited UK electrical engineering programs, including Russell Group universities and accredited technical colleges.

How current is the verification data?

Our system receives monthly updates directly from participating institutions. Typically, graduations are entered within 30 days of degree conferral.

What information is displayed when verifying a certificate?

The verification displays: graduate's name, institution, degree/diploma title, graduation date, certificate number, and status (active/revoked).
